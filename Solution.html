Here's how it works

1

Real-Time Soil Health Monitoring

The core of our solution is a network of advanced IoT sensors placed directly in the soil. These sensors continuously monitor key soil health parameters, including:

  • Nitrate levels
  • Soil moisture
  • pH levels
  • Temperature

By constantly gathering this data, we provide farmers with a real-time view of their soil's health, enabling them to make data-driven decisions about fertilization, irrigation, and crop management.

2

The Power of Predictive Analytics

Our Predictive Analytics Engine, powered by machine learning (ML), is designed to analyze both historical and real-time data. It combines information from our soil sensors, weather patterns, pest activity, and other relevant factors to provide farmers with actionable insights.

This predictive capability means farmers can forecast optimal fertilization schedules, predict irrigation needs, and receive alerts for potential pest risks or other threats to crop health.

3

Data-Driven Fertilization Recommendations

Over-fertilization is one of the major contributors to environmental pollution, but with AgriSenze's system, farmers can apply the right amount of fertilizer at the right time. Our system provides precise recommendations on fertilizer types and quantities based on real-time soil data.

This not only helps save money by reducing unnecessary fertilizer costs, but it also ensures optimal crop health and better yields.

4

Mobile & Web Dashboard for Easy Access

All the data gathered by our sensors is integrated into an intuitive, user-friendly dashboard, accessible via both mobile and web apps. Through this dashboard, farmers can monitor soil health in real-time, track historical data, get actionable alerts, and visualize predictive insights.

Whether in the field or in the office, farmers can make informed, data-backed decisions at any time.
